Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

We keep this simple. We collect only what we need, use it only for what we say, and never sell it. Full stop.

1. Who We Are

Mon Élevage is a wine and technology consultancy registered as a sole proprietorship in Singapore. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to Mon Élevage.

For any privacy-related queries, contact us at hello@monelevagesg.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect personal data that you choose to provide to us directly, through:

  • Contact form — name, email address, company name, and your message
  • Demo request form — name, email address, company, role, project description, and timeline
  • Email correspondence — any information you share when you email us directly

We do not use tracking pixels, third-party analytics, advertising cookies, or behavioural tracking of any kind on this website.

3. How We Use It

We use your personal data solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or demo request
  • Prepare and send a proposal if relevant
  • Communicate with you in the course of an engagement

We will never use your data for unsolicited marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond the reason you shared it with us.

4. Who We Share It With

No one. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

Your data may pass through the following infrastructure services solely to facilitate delivery:

  • Cloudflare — website hosting and email routing
  • Google — email inbox (Gmail), used to receive and respond to messages

Both are used only for delivery and storage — neither is granted access to use your data independently.

5. How Long We Keep It

We retain your information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your enquiry or fulfil an engagement. If we determine no ongoing relationship exists, we delete correspondence data within 12 months.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@monelevagesg.com.

6. Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics. No cookie consent banner is necessary because no such cookies are set.

7. Your Rights

Under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent to our use of your data
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@monelevagesg.com. We will respond within 10 business days.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact

Questions, requests, or concerns about this policy can be directed to:
