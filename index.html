About

Sommelier
meets
Product Mind

Mon Élevage is a wine and technology consultancy founded on a simple observation: the wine industry has extraordinary craft, and an extraordinary gap in how that craft is built, sold, and experienced digitally.

We sit at that intersection — fluent in both the language of the cellar and the logic of the product roadmap. From wine program design to digital product builds, every engagement is guided by the same philosophy: deliberate, unhurried, purposeful.

The name is a nod to élevage — the winemaker's art of raising a wine to its fullest expression. We hold that same standard for every project we take on.
